隐私政策 — Game Dev Simulator

更新日期：2026年3月

概述

本隐私政策由 Jasper Seed（Nathan Barnes）（以下简称"我们"）制定，说明我们在您使用 Game Dev Simulator 及相关服务（以下统称"本服务"）时如何处理您的个人信息。请您在使用本服务前仔细阅读并理解本隐私政策。

This Privacy Policy is prepared by Jasper Seed (Nathan Barnes) to explain how we handle your personal information when you use Game Dev Simulator and related services. Please carefully read and understand this Privacy Policy before using this service.

1. 我们收集的个人信息

本服务不收集、存储、处理或传输任何个人信息。所有游戏数据（包括存档和设置）均仅存储在您的本地设备上，不会传输至任何服务器或第三方。

This service does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information. All game data (including saves and settings) is stored solely on your local device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

2. 权限情况

本服务不请求任何敏感设备权限（如摄像头、麦克风、位置信息等）。本服务仅使用 VR 头显和控制器的标准 OpenXR 输入功能。

This service does not request any sensitive device permissions (such as camera, microphone, or location). The service only uses standard OpenXR input from the VR headset and controllers.

3. 个人信息的使用

由于我们不收集任何个人信息，因此不存在使用您的个人信息的情形。

Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no circumstances in which your personal information is used.

4. 共享和公开披露个人信息

我们承诺，不会主动共享、转让或公开披露您的个人信息至任何第三方，因为我们不收集任何个人信息。本服务不包含任何第三方 SDK、分析工具或广告服务。

We will not share, transfer, or publicly disclose your personal information to any third party, as we do not collect any personal information. This service does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising services.

5. 个人信息的存储

本服务的所有数据（包括游戏存档和设置）仅存储在您的本地设备上。没有任何数据被传输至外部服务器或云端。

All data for this service (including game saves and settings) is stored solely on your local device. No data is transmitted to external servers or the cloud.

6. 您的权益

由于本服务不收集任何个人信息，您的个人信息权利不受影响。您可以随时通过卸载本应用来删除所有本地存储的数据。

Since this service does not collect any personal information, your personal information rights are not affected. You may delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the application.

7. 投诉与反馈

若您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式与我们联系：jasper@jasperseed.com。我们将在15个工作日内回复您的请求。

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: jasper@jasperseed.com. We will respond to your request within 15 business days.

8. 其他

本服务不专门面向未成年人。本隐私政策可能会不时更新。任何重大变更将通过应用更新通知您。

This service is not specifically directed at minors. This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated through application updates.
